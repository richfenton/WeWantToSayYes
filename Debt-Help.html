If you are struggling with your financial affairs and need help support and advice, we recommend contacting one of the agencies opposite, Remember you do not have to cope alone and all of these organisations offer their services free of charge.

Money Helper are an independent service, set up by government to help people make the most of their money, they give free, unbiased money advice to everyone across the UK - online, over the phone and face to face.

The Citizens Advice Bureau provide free legal, independent, confidential and impartial advice to everyone on their rights and responsibilities. Citizens Advice Bureau deliver advice services from over 3,500 community locations in England and Wales

Step Change deal with clients with a range of debt problems. Your first step should always be to get free expert debt advice, based on your personal circumstances. No matter what your debt problem, they will be able to help. Over the last twenty years, they've helped more than two million people, many of whom have faced exactly the same kind of situation as you.
